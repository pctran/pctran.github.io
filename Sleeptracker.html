Sleeptracker

Full Download

Developed in Ionic 4, the purpose of this application is to allow users to log their sleep and sleepiness throughout the day.

Home:


Home page shows previously logged data in cards. Users can add more data with the plus button.

Logging Data:


From this page, users can log their overnight sleep, their sleepiness throughout the day, and set timed notifications to remind the user to log their data.


Modules:


Adding data uses familiar DateTime modules.
